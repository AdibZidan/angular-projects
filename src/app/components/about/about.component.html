Final Project - Humanitarian clothing portal

I had the opportunity to work with 6 wonderful people and we created an application called Apparel.

The team consisted of 7 total members; 2 Product Owners, 3 Back-End & 2 Front-End developers.

Apparel is a web-application where a humanitarian organization can offer apparel to those in need.

The humanitarian organization receives donated clothing and the users of the application can select the clothing they need from the catalogue and mark them as reserved.

The users can then go to the apparel warehouse and receive them.

In the future, volunteers could sign up to deliver these apparels to the location of the person in need via geolocation.

It was developed with a microservice infrastructure and Scrum.
